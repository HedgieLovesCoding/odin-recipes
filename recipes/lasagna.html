Kung Pao Chicken

INGREDIENTS

STIR FRY

  • 500g chicken thigh fillet, cut medium
  • 1 tbsp ground white pepper
  • 7 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 bawang merah, sliced
  • 1/2 red capsicum, cubed
  • 1/2 green capsicum, cubed
  • 2 cili kering, halved (optional)

SAUCE

  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 3 tbsp sambal oelek
  • 5 tbsp kicap manis
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, roasted
  • 1/2 cube chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Marinate chicken with salt and pepper. Put aside while prepping other ingredients.
  2. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Panaskan minyak. Stir fry bawang merah, capsciums and cili kering until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken. Stir fry until cooked through (turns white).
  5. Add sauce, cashew nuts and chicken stock. Simmer until sauce thickens like syrup. Adjust with kicap/brown sugar if need to.
  6. Serve.
